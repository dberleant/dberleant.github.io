What is PathBinder?
  • A repository of sentences…
    • containing 2 protein names
    • heirarchically browsable (like Yahoo)
    • mined from “all” of MEDLINE
    • with a protein synonym list
      • (mined mostly from SwissProt)
    • …a work in progress
3
Status
  • Goal: a static Web directory of mined sentences
    • Should have all relevant sentences in MEDLINE
    • Should have Yahoo-style drill-down browsing
    • Given 2 biochemicals, user obtains list of…
      • …all sentences containing both biochemicals
    • Click sentence to get abstract from external source
  • Current size is 11 GBytes
  • URL is www.vrac.iastate.edu/~berleant/MedRep

Phrases, Sentences or Abstracts? An investigation
  • What text unit is best for interaction extraction?
  • Information retrieval metrics were used:
    • Precision, Recall, and Effectiveness
  • Phrases win on precision
  • Abstracts win on recall
  • Sentences win on effectiveness
  • Paper presented at PSB 2002
9
Why not Phrases?
  • If focus is on precision, phrases are better
  • A phrase version of PathBinder:
    • Straightforward to mine from current version
    • Delete sentence co-occurrences that are not also phrase co-occurences
  • PathBinder could also be converted to other forms as well
10
Distances and Patterns:
an Investigation
  • Two biochemical terms in a sentence…
    • …may or may not interact
  • We found that probability of interaction
    • …is affected by
      • how near/distant the terms are
      • presence, location of relevant verb(s)
  • Paper is currently under revision
  • PathBinder could be annotated accordingly
11
Verbs: an Investigation
  • Certain words (usually verbs)
    • Are used to indicate interactions
    • Some words are better indicators than others
  • We call these words interactors
    • (because they are not always verbs)
  • We have raw data on relative abilities of different words to indicate interactions
    • We plan to analyze the data
  • PathBinder could be updated accordingly

PathBinder: Future Paths
  • Making a phrase repository
    • (in IR terms we get higher precision, lower recall)
  • Mining out subsets
    • (e.g. based on species, interaction, precision, etc.)
  • Transforming into database format
    • (for use by pathway simulating systems, etc.)
  • Make available as an online literature search tool
    • (more efficient searches = more efficient research)
  • Add ontology component
    • (UCP1 is a UCP; raspberry is a bramble)
  • Annotate sentences with their quality
    • (Easier lit. searches, more accurate simulation)
