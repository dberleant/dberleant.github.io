The Approach
·Distribution Envelope Determination (DED)
·Problem inputs
·Arbitrary marginals U and V
·A function g(sU,sV)=sX 
·sU, sV are samples of U, V
·sX are samples of derived distribution X
·Problem outputs
·Envelopes around distribution (cdf) of X
·U and V independent – easy, use convolution
·U and V have unknown dependency – use DED
·U and V have known correlation – use DED
