This is a communication device designed to help a child with Down’s Syndrome to communicate with family and friends with the aid of visual and pre-recorded audio messages.  Based on a Gameboy unit. Involved extensive re-programming of the device, which does support software development and has an on-line community of developers.
Designed to provide children with limited coordination and mobility with a device that will aid them in the operation of switches that you and I encounter everyday and take for granted.  Large, accessible switch remotely controls a device conveniently placed over an ordinary wall switch using velcro strips.
