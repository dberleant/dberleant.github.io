Interaction defined here as:
A direct or indirect influence of one on
the quantity or activity of the other.
Interaction examples
A increased B.
A activated C, and C activated B.
A-induced increase in C is mediated through B.
Inhibition of C by A can be blocked by an inhibitor of B.
Non-interaction examples
A increases C, and B also increases C.
C decreases A and B.
