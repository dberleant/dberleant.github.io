Gemini.google.com:

Me: pronounce charAt() in JS

Gemini: The JavaScript method charAt() is pronounced "char at".

·         "char" is pronounced like "character" (or like 'car' with a 'sh' sound in front), as it's the common abbreviation for character.

·         "at" is pronounced like the English preposition "at".

In another threadI suggested pronouncing “character” as “chair-ick-ter” and Gemini agreed this is fine.
