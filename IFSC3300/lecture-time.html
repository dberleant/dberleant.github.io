Time

UTC is a worldwide time standard.

UTC = Coordinated Universal Time.

Why UTC, not CUT? There is no clean and simple answer!

See: Wikipedia: Coordinated Universal Time

Time zones are defined as offsets from UTC:

  • Central Time Zone
    • Map: Wikipedia: Central Time Zone
    • Uses Central Daylight Time (UTC−5) — Spring ahead!
    • Uses Central Standard Time (UTC−6) — Fall back!

Daylight Saving Time:

  • Proposed in 1895 by George Hudson in New Zealand.
  • Used by many countries — e.g. New Zealand.
  • Not used by many countries.
  • Has been found to be unhealthy.
  • Recommendation: use sunrise-linked local times!
