Lifelong Learning & AI Prompting

  • Computer technology changes fast!
  • What you learn today might not be as useful in a few years
  • New tech will appear even after you graduate
  • Solution: "lifelong learning"
  • AI makes a convenient tutor
  • When you say something to an AI, it is called a "prompt"
  • Prompting skills are valuable to develop
  • AI follows directions, so you can ask it to:
    • "Make your answer brief"
    • "Even briefer this time"
    • "Answer at a beginner level"
    • "Answer at an intermediate level"
    • "Answer at an easier (or more advanced) level this time"
  • AI will not always be correct
  • If you feel like you understand more, you probably do!
Caution: This is not always true. Clever manipulators can sometimes make people "understand" ideas that benefit the manipulator, not the target. Misinformation theory is a topic for another course.
